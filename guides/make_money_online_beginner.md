# How to Make Money Online as a Complete Beginner

**Welcome!**
If you’ve never made money online before, this guide is for you. Follow each step carefully.

## Step 1: Understand the Basics
- Making money online means you earn income using the internet—no special skills needed to start.
- You can promote products, offer services, or share referral links.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Payment (Stripe)
- Make sure your Stripe account is upgraded and connected (see Guide 1 above).
- This is how you’ll get paid.

## Step 3: Get Your Affiliate Link
- Log in to your back office.
- Find your unique referral or affiliate link. This is the link you’ll share to earn commissions.

## Step 4: Share Your Link
**Where to share:**
- Social media (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter)
- Messaging apps (WhatsApp, Messenger, Telegram)
- Email (send to friends/family)
- Your own website or blog (if you have one)

**How to share:**
- Write a short message about the product or service.
- Example:
  “Check out this amazing AI tool! Sign up here: [your link]”

**Tips:**
- Be honest and friendly.
- Don’t spam—share with people who might be interested.

## Step 5: Learn and Improve
- Read the other guides (Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, etc.).
- Try different messages and platforms to see what works best.

## Step 6: Get Paid
- When someone uses your link and makes a purchase, you earn a commission.
- Stripe will send your earnings to your bank account automatically.

---
**Remember:**
- Everyone starts as a beginner.
- Be patient and consistent—results come with time and effort.
- Ask for help in your back office if you get stuck!
